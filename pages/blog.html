About Us

Floral Nepal is a unique business that combines a passion for nature with a commitment to environmental conservation and sustainability. The company aims to provide high-quality plants and products to its customers, while also contributing to the conservation of natural habitats and promoting sustainable practices.
Floral Nepal is also dedicated to raising awareness about the importance of protecting the environment through various programs and initiatives. The company specializes in selling a wide range of plants and fertilizers, as well as offering services such as afforestation and clean environment preservation.
